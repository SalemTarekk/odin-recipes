Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Water (for sauce)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg (for binding in the cheese layer)

instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to the specified temperature.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain.
  3. Brown the ground beef or Italian sausage in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add chopped onion and garlic to the meat, cooking until the onion is softened.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, salt, pepper, dried oregano, and dried basil. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and an egg. Mix well.
  7. In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, cooked lasagna noodles, and the cheese mixture.
  8. Repeat the layering until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  9. Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  11. Allow the lasagna to rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
  12. Slice into portions and serve. Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
